## My Ideal Week

**Admin / email / Salesforce:** Before 9am and after 5pm — protect the 9–5 window for high-value work

**Between 9am–5pm (when not on calls):**
- Prospecting — outbound, LinkedIn, working with William on target accounts
- Highest-value deal activities — multi-threading, strategizing, inviting prospects to events, advancing deals forward
- No low-value admin during core hours if it can be done before 9am or after 5pm

**External / prospect calls:** Anytime between 9am–5pm
**Deep work (deal strategy, research, writing):** Best done in focused blocks between meetings
