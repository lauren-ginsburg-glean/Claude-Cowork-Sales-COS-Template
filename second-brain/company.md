## What Glean Does

Glean is the AI-powered work assistant that helps enterprises find information, synthesize knowledge, and get answers from across all their company's apps and data — instantly.

Glean connects to 100+ enterprise applications (Slack, Salesforce, Confluence, Google Drive, Jira, etc.) and uses AI to surface the right information to the right person at the right time — without them having to know where to look.

**One-line pitch:**
> "Glean gives every employee a single place to search, ask, and get answers from everything your company knows."
